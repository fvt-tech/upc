About Us

Unleashed Potential Consult is an innovative and modern football management agency providing personal representation services to professional footballers and athletes from Ghana and across the globe. Unleashed Potential Consult and its dedicated team of professionals are licensed in Ghana to carry out player transfers and player scouting services.

Each of our clients is supplied with an outstanding and comprehensive range of personalized services, tailored to meet their individual needs both on and off the pitch.

As one of football’s fastest growing management agencies, Unleashed Potential Consult is building a leading reputation for professionalism, integrity and client satisfaction.

Our dedicated team here at Unleashed Potential Consult is all experienced former professional sports and business executives. This experience makes us understand the requirements of our clients on a day to day basis, the importance to prepare, to succeed now and for the future.

Unleashed Potential Consult is well qualified to negotiate at all levels with football clubs and player contracts, whilst dealing in lucrative commercial opportunities for their clients.

MISSION STATEMENT:

To provide solutions for challenges in the football industry in Ghana as well as quality representation of our clients, through creative marketing ideas, education and financial planning.

VISION:

To be the leading sports management and agency entity in Ghana and the African region.

Contract Negotiation and Legal Advice

Unleashed Potential Consult (UPC) offers its clients top quality contract negotiation and legal advice. We ensure that our client’s bottom line is maximized each and every time. At UPC we place emphasis on this component of our management service. Contract negotiation is where we really make a difference, ensuring we nail the best all round package from our client’s primary income source. In addition to contract negotiation, we also offer legal advice for personal and professional matters

Financial Planning

As players move up professionally, incomes increase and extra money may be available. That is the stage we formulate personalized financial advises for our clients. We develop a plan of action to achieve realistic and successful financial goals. We analyze your financial situation, then advice and develop strategies that best suit your personal situation; these include introducing our clients to a wide variety of investment options, setting up medical aids, retirement annuities, property investments, Business Opportunities and more.

Post Career Planning

Unleashed Potential Consult will work closely with its clients to ensure they have a post career plan. Most players cannot afford to retire after their careers and it is therefore essential that each client has a plan of action. Unleashed Potential Consult will assist all its clients in pursuing whatever career path they choose after they have completed their careers. We will assist with study options, business setup, franchise acquisitions, property investing and introduce players to corporates who could potentially employ the client in the future.

All Unleashed Potential Consult junior clients will receive bursaries to further their education and we will ensure the player continues to study and effectively has “something to fall back on” once he ends his playing career. Unleashed Potential Consult will also begin to “Coach” its clients early in their career, to prepare them for the “normal” working world. It is important to make a smooth transition from a successful athletic career to a successful post athletic endeavor. We will help our clients build relationships that will help them get started and advise them regarding their new career path. Bottom line, we care and want our players post-career dreams to become realities.

Overseas Contract Opportunities

UPC has major links in Europe and major Asian Leagues which ensure our clients have the choice of furthering their careers abroad. UPC has associate agents abroad who will ensure our clients are well “looked after” when in foreign countries. Our associates will be readily available to continue servicing our clients as if they were still in Ghana. UPC will also prepare its clients to travel to their new clubs; information packs on the new country, the town and club as well as Language Lessons.
